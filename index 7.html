About

15 years inside the rooms that matter.

I’m Richelle — a leadership coach and culture consultant with deep roots in HR at Meta, Amazon, and Netflix. I’ve sat in the room where culture is made and broken, and I’ve watched brilliant technical leaders plateau not because of skill gaps, but because no one ever taught them how to lead people.

Coral Collaborative exists to change that. Coral looks delicate — but it’s one of the most resilient structures on earth. The leaders I work with are the same: quietly strong, built to last, capable of holding entire ecosystems together.

My approach is grounded in real organizational experience, not theory. I seek to understand complex issues deeply before offering perspective, and I hold space for honest, direct conversations — because that’s where the real work happens.
