About

15 years inside the rooms that matter.

I'm Richelle — a leadership coach and culture consultant with deep roots in HR at Meta, Amazon, and Netflix. I've been in the rooms where careers are shaped, cultures are made or broken, and people figure out who they want to become as leaders and professionals.

Coral Collaborative exists to bring that experience directly to you — wherever you are in your growth. Whether you're a professional at a crossroads, a leader navigating complexity, or a team ready to unlock the next level, the work meets you where you are.

Coral looks delicate — but it's one of the most resilient structures on earth. The people I work with are the same: quietly strong, built to last, capable of holding entire ecosystems together when given the right conditions.

As organizations race to scale with AI, I believe that human work matters more than ever. The capacity to lead with clarity, build trust, and bring out the best in people isn't something you can automate — and it's precisely what separates good organizations from great ones.

My approach is grounded in real organizational experience, not theory. I hold space for honest, direct conversations — because that's where the real growth happens.
